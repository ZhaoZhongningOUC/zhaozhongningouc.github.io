TransFish

TransFish: day-level forecasting of fishing effort distribution via transformer on multi-source data

Fuses historical fishing distributions, ocean hydrology, and chlorophyll data with ResNet and Transformer to forecast day-level fishing effort for the coming week.

5.32% average error

HiTrip

HiTrip: Historical trajectory interpolation for trawlers via deep learning on multi-source data

Reconstructs two-hour historical vessel tracks at three-minute resolution by combining VMS records with ocean hydrological fields.

0.20 km East China Sea error

Fishing Chronology

Short-term prediction of fishing effort distributions by discovering fishing chronology among trawlers based on VMS dataset

Introduces fishing chronology to identify a small indicator fleet whose current behavior predicts near-future fishing-effort distributions.

19 / 1,589 indicator vessels · 6.95% error
